Loss of Precision

Define the absolute error as $$ \begin{equation} |fl(a)-a|, \tag{11} \end{equation} $$ whereas the relative error is $$ \begin{equation} \frac{ |fl(a)-a|}{a} \le \epsilon_a. \tag{12} \end{equation} $$
